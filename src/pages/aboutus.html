SafeVoices

Empowering Women, Amplifying Voices, Ending Violence

Our Mission and Vision

Our mission is to empower women to reclaim their voices, ensure justice for survivors, and contribute to a future free from gender-based violence in Sierra Leone.

We envision a world where every woman feels safe, heard, and supported, with access to the resources she needs to thrive.

Our Story

SafeVoices was born out of the urgent need to address the rising cases of gender-based violence in Sierra Leone. After hearing countless stories of women struggling in silence, we realized the importance of creating a digital safe space where survivors could report incidents without fear of stigma or retaliation. Today, SafeVoices stands as a testament to the power of community-driven solutions and the resilience of women.

Our Core Values

  • Confidentiality: We protect your information and ensure it remains private.
  • Empathy: We provide a compassionate, non-judgmental space for survivors.
  • Empowerment: We help women reclaim their voices and agency.
  • Justice: We advocate for the rights and safety of all women.
  • Community: We work hand-in-hand with organizations and individuals to create change.

What We Do

SafeVoices provides a secure platform for women in Sierra Leone to report gender-based violence, access resources, and find support. Our services include:

  • Reporting Gender-Based Violence: Provide a secure, user-friendly form for survivors to share their stories, anonymously or identified.
  • Access to Resources: Connect women with local shelters, legal aid, counseling, and more.
  • Awareness Campaigns: Educate communities about the causes, effects, and prevention of gender-based violence.
  • Advocacy: Work with stakeholders to push for systemic changes and better protections for women.

Who We Serve

SafeVoices is dedicated to supporting women of all ages, backgrounds, and circumstances who are survivors of gender-based violence. Whether you are seeking immediate help, long-term resources, or simply a listening ear, we are here for you.
