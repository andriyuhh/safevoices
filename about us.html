About Us

SafeVoices SL is a platform dedicated to giving a voice to those affected by gender-based violence in Sierra Leone. Our mission is to provide a safe, confidential, and accessible way for victims to report incidents, receive support, and connect with the necessary services to help them heal and seek justice.

Our Mission

At SafeVoices SL, our mission is clear: to empower individuals by providing a safe space to report gender-based violence and access comprehensive support. We aim to promote justice, safety, and healing by ensuring that every voice is heard, regardless of identity or status.

Our Core Values

  • Confidentiality: We ensure that all reports and personal information are handled with the utmost discretion and security.
  • Empowerment: We strive to empower survivors by giving them control over their stories and the reporting process.
  • Accessibility: Our platform is designed to be inclusive, offering support for people with disabilities and multi-language options, including Krio.
  • Support: We are committed to connecting survivors with legal, medical, and psychological resources to help them through their journey.
  • Justice: We believe in seeking justice for victims of gender-based violence by working with local authorities and advocacy groups.
